
POND - the Petz Offset Number Directory - is located at
http://zeva.sphosting.com/pond/
I did write to them with my list of breedz numbers a couple of months back, 
but the owner seems to have stopped updating; as I write this (October 2003), 
the latest date there is in July of this year.  So I thought, why waste my
efforts, what the heck, here is the list for you all to see :-)

I've hexed a few breedz over the years; most of mine are using the
"PetzGallery Standard" numbers, for which I was allocated the whole of
the series in which the second half of the number was 09.  Then, when I
had filled up that whole set with conversions of breedz (I helped out at
the PetzGallery back then, and there were _hundreds_ of conversions), I
was allocated the whole of set 40.  Anyway, here is the list of my own
breedz, it's pretty much complete as far as I can recall and as accurate
as I can make it.  There may be some missing, I've been at this hexing
business for wa-ay too long and I've made something like 200 breedz or
so.  Some of the breedz have the same numbers as others, this is
deliberate.  There was one oopsie, the Dalmatian Cat which has ended up
out there with two different ID numbers.  I think that's the only such
oopsie 8-)

On the POND site the owner says to "send in Dogz 1, Catz 1, Petz 2, 
Petz 3, Petz 4, and Petz 5 breed offsets".  There were no such things as 
offsets before Petz 3; so you'll only get offsets for Petz 3, 4 and 5.  
Oddballz is a completely different matter, and a list for those breedz 
is being maintained at Daniel's Oddballz Neo.

I hope this list is helpful.  They are all breedz that I made for Petz
3, 4 and 5 up to September 2003.
